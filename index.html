I recieved my verified bot developer tag in 2017 (1,000 servers using your bot) and was hooked. I loved making any idea I had and spent many many days doing it. I eventually got deeper and deeper into software and moved over to FiveM scripts via C# (mostly private scripts) and played around with .LUA for the same platform. I tested out RUST which I liked as a lang but never got deeper into due to constraints on the amount of projects I already had. I am fully self taught orginally learning through Youtube for Discord Bots then carrying into writing fully independent of anyone. Xen is no longer active due to my lack of time and my buddy has moved into his own company which I help when it's needed. My dream goal has always been to land into a development job anywhere as it's my passion.
